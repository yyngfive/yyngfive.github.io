The Apple Logo

苹果公司的logo

Apple's logo is one of the most familiar icons around the world. But what's the story about the Apple logo? The image of the apple is obvious,because that's the name of the company. What about the bite?

苹果公司的logo是世界上最被人熟知的logo之一。 但苹果公司的logo有什么故事呢？ 苹果的图像容易让人理解，因为那是苹果公司的名字。 但咬了一口是怎么回事？

图为苹果公司的logo

The famous story is that the logo shows respect and honour for Alan Turing. He was the father of computer science. He researched AI(artificial intelligence) and unlocked German wartime codes. However,after the war,he was put into prison,because of his beliefs. He could not stand the looking down and chose to bite a poisoned apple to end his life in the prison.

一个著名的故事说，这个logo是为了表达对Alan Turing（图灵）的尊重。 他是计算机科学之父。 他研究AI（人工智能）并且破译了德军的战时密码。 不过，战争之后（译：二战后）他因信仰（译：指同性恋）的问题被关进了监狱。 他无法忍受压力，在监狱中，用毒苹果结束了自己的一生。

However ,Rob Janoff,who designed the famous Apple logo,said,"It is not the truth,but only a legend. The real reason why I designed it with a bite is very simple . I wanted people to get that it was an apple not a cherry. When I go to markets to buy apples,I always mistake apples for cherries. It really has nothing to do with the scientist."

但是，设计了苹果logo的著名设计师——Rob Janoff说：“这不是事实，只不过是一个传说。 我把它设计成被咬了一口的真正原因是很简单。 我想让人们明白这是一个苹果，不是樱桃。 我去市场买苹果时，我总把樱桃和苹果搞混。 真的和那位科学家没有关系。”

Are you kidding? An apple is much bigger than a cherry.It is very easy to tell. But if you use an aple to design a logo,some people may mistake it for a cherry.

开玩笑的吧？ 苹果可比樱桃大多了。很好辨认。 但是如果使用苹果来设计一个logo，一些人可能会错把它当作樱桃。
